With this app you can explore the population of Ukraine. All data is gathered in 2016.

To use this app, click on the circle on the map and you will see information with name of the choisen region and its population. Also you can select region in the right corner of the map using drop-down menu and also explore the population of selected region.

Lastly, if you move the mouse round pie chart in the right corner you can compare the percentage of population in each region.
